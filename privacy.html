Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Draft for attorney review before publication. Confirm the business mailing address and data practices are accurate for your operation.

Solar Power by Taylor, operated by Praos Services, LLC ("we," "us"), respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use our website or request a solar estimate, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you submit our estimate request form, we collect the information you provide: your name, mobile phone number, email address, and home address. We may also collect basic technical information such as your device and browser type and general location through standard web analytics.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to contact you about a solar estimate and related follow-up, to respond to your questions, and to operate and improve our website. We contact you by phone, text message, and email using the contact details you provide.

Text messaging and mobile opt-in

If you consent to receive text messages, we send messages related to your solar estimate. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help. We do not sell or share your mobile opt-in information or the consent you provide with any third parties for their own marketing purposes.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate — for example, our hosting, messaging, and customer-management vendors — and only so they can perform services for us. We may share a lead with a licensed solar installer or financing provider in order to prepare the estimate you requested; we do not share your mobile opt-in data for that party's own marketing. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Your California privacy rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request that we delete or correct it, and to opt out of any sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at solarpowerbytaylor@gmail.com or (559) 961-0080. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights, and we respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: We do not sell or share your personal information. If our practices change, we will provide a working opt-out method here.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide you an estimate and follow up, and as required for our legal and business records, after which we delete or de-identify it.
